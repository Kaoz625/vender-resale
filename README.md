# Vender Resale — NY State Prison Package Vendor

NY DOCCS Directive 4911 compliant resale business selling food, clothing, and hygiene products to incarcerated individuals in New York State facilities.

**Email:** vender@nyctailblazers.com  
**Domain:** nyctailblazers.com  
**GitHub:** https://github.com/Kaoz625/vender-resale

## What We Sell
- Food (commercially/hermetically sealed — meats, snacks, beverages, grocery staples)
- Clothing (sweatsuits, thermals, socks, undergarments)
- Hygiene / Personal Care (alcohol-free deodorants, soaps, lotions, oral care)

## Key Rules (Directive 4911)
- Max 3 packages/month per inmate, 40 lbs combined
- All food must be commercially or hermetically sealed — no homemade
- No glass, cans, metal containers — plastic/vacuum-sealed only
- No alcohol-containing products (except stearyl alcohol)
- No poppy seeds, smart devices, or items over 16 oz single unit
- Ship via USPS, UPS, or FedEx only — no direct facility drop-off
