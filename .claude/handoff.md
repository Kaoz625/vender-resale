## Email Routing — Manual Setup (2 min)
1. Go to dash.cloudflare.com → click nyctailblazers.com
2. Left sidebar → Email → Email Routing
3. Click "Enable Email Routing" if not already on
4. Click "Add address" → Custom address: vender → Forward to: (your catchall destination)
5. Save — done
