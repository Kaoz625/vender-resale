## Differentiation Opportunities

- **Local NY brand** — several competitors are out of state
- **Buy-now-pay-later** — Wilson does it, others don't — families often need this
- **Diabetic/health-conscious options** — underserved niche
- **Subscription / auto-ship** — predictable revenue
- **Spanish-language site** — large Latino inmate population in NY
- **Same-day processing** — most take 7–10 days
- **Bundle deals** — pre-curated monthly packages simplify ordering for families
