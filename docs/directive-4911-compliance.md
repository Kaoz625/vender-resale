# NY DOCCS Directive 4911 — Compliance Guide

## What Directive 4911 Covers

Directive 4911 governs all packages and articles sent to NY State DOCCS facilities. Directive 4911A (the Secure Vendor Program) requires that packages come **directly from approved commercial vendors** — families can no longer ship packages directly (with rare exceptions: wedding ring, release clothing, non-electric musical instruments).

**This means: every inmate's family must use a vendor like us to send packages.**

---

## Allowed Product Categories

| Category | Notes |
|---|---|
| Food | Commercially/hermetically sealed only. Cooked/cured/smoked meats vacuum-sealed. Coffee, tea, drink mixes, snacks. |
| Clothing | Sweatsuits, thermals, socks, undergarments, footwear |
| Hygiene / Personal Care | Alcohol-free only. Deodorant, soap, lotion, shampoo, oral care. |
| Bedding | Blankets must be fire-retardant |
| Electronics | Non-smart, clear housing only. Wired headphones/earbuds. |
| Books / Games / Art | With facility superintendent approval |
| Religious Items | Approved by facility |

---

## Hard No's (Will Get You Banned)

- Glass, cans, or metal containers
- Any alcohol-containing products (except stearyl alcohol)
- Poppy seeds
- Marshmallows
- Cellphones or smart devices
- Smart watches
- Hair coloring / skin tanning products
- Single items over 16 oz
- Homemade food of any kind

---

## Package Limits Per Inmate

- **3 packages per month maximum**
- **40 lbs combined weight**
- **8 lbs max food** in a 30 lb package
- Ship via USPS, UPS, or FedEx only
- No direct facility drop-off

---

## Vendor Approval Process

**There is NO approved vendor list.** Instead, DOCCS maintains a **Disapproved Vendor List**.

- If you are NOT on the disapproved list → you can operate
- You get added to the disapproved list by repeatedly sending contraband or violating Directive 4911
- Always check the list before launching: [March 2026 Disapproved List](https://doccs.ny.gov/system/files/documents/2026/03/incarcerated-individual-disapproved-package-vendor-list-3-10-2026.pdf)

---

## Business Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| NY State Business License | Register entity with Secretary of State |
| Sales Tax Permit | NY Dept of Taxation and Finance |
| Workers' Comp Insurance | Required. Use Form C-105.2 (private carrier) or U-26.3 (State Insurance Fund) |
| Resale Certificate | Needed to buy wholesale without paying sales tax |
| Background Check | Disclose criminal convictions to NY Dept of State — violations can trigger disapproval |
| Bonding | Required for formal DOCCS contracts — contact DOCCS for amount |
